## TIER 1 DRIVER ONBOARDING (COMPANY FLEET)

### üìã PRE-ONBOARDING (Before First Day)

#### Documents Collection
- [ ] **Driving License** (Original + Photocopy)
  - Minimum 3 years experience
  - Valid for at least 12 months
  - No serious violations in last 2 years
  
- [ ] **Aadhaar Card** (Original + Photocopy)
  
- [ ] **PAN Card** (Photocopy)
  
- [ ] **Address Proof** (Electricity bill / Rent agreement - within 3 months)
  
- [ ] **Police Verification Certificate** (or initiate process)
  - Local police station verification
  - Takes 7-15 days
  
- [ ] **2 Passport Size Photos**
  
- [ ] **Bank Account Details**
  - Cancelled cheque OR
  - Bank statement (first page)
  - UPI ID
  
- [ ] **Emergency Contact Details**
  - Name, relationship, phone number
  
- [ ] **References** (2 people)
  - Name, phone, occupation, how they know driver

#### Background Verification
- [ ] **Driving Record Check**
  - Request driving history from RTO
  - Check for DUI, reckless driving, serious violations
  
- [ ] **Criminal Background Check**
  - Police verification initiated
  - Reference calls completed
  
- [ ] **Previous Employment Verification** (if applicable)
  - Call previous employer (if Ola/Uber/other taxi driver)
  - Ask about: Reliability, behavior, reason for leaving

#### Medical Fitness
- [ ] **Basic Health Check**
  - Vision test (can read number plate from 20 meters)
  - No color blindness
  - No hearing impairment
  
- [ ] **Fitness Certificate** (if driver age >50 years)
  
- [ ] **Covid Vaccination Certificate** (recommended)

#### Contract & Legal
- [ ] **Driver Service Agreement** signed (Tier 1 contract)
  
- [ ] **Insurance Nominee Form** filled
  - For health insurance (family members)
  - For personal accident coverage
  
- [ ] **Photo Consent Form** signed
  - Permission to use photo in app/marketing
  
- [ ] **Data Privacy Acknowledgment** signed

## TIER 2 DRIVER ONBOARDING (FREELANCE)

### Simplified Process (Self-Service with Support)

#### Step 1: Online Registration
- [ ] Driver submits form on website
  - Name, phone, email
  - License number
  - Vehicle registration
  - Insurance details
  
- [ ] Admin reviews (24 hours)
  
- [ ] Approval/Rejection notification

#### Step 2: Document Verification (Video Call)
- [ ] 30-minute video call with admin
  
- [ ] Driver shows original documents on camera:
  - Driving license
  - Vehicle RC
  - Insurance certificate
  - PAN card
  - Aadhaar card
  
- [ ] Documents photographed and verified

#### Step 3: Payment Setup
- [ ] First month subscription payment (‚Çπ999)
  
- [ ] Bank account details submitted for settlements
  
- [ ] UPI ID verified

#### Step 4: App Access & Training
- [ ] Driver app login credentials sent
  
- [ ] Video training link shared (30 minutes)
  - App usage
  - Accepting rides
  - Charging network access
  - Rating system
  - Payment process
  
- [ ] Online quiz (pass = 15+/20)

#### Step 5: Go Live
- [ ] Driver status activated
  
- [ ] First ride assigned (with admin monitoring)
  
- [ ] Welcome email/SMS with FAQs
  
- [ ] Added to driver WhatsApp support group

**Ongoing:**
- [ ] Weekly performance reports via email
- [ ] Monthly subscription auto-debit
- [ ] Support via WhatsApp/Email/Phone
